Delete Account Request

If you would like to delete your DataNurse account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

📧 support@datanurse.io

Please send an email including the following details:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your username (if applicable)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
⚠️ Once your account is deleted, all your data including medical records, reports, and profile information will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

Your request will be processed within 5 working days.

If you have any issues, feel free to contact our support team.
