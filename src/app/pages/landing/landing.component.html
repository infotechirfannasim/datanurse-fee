About DataNurse

Built for Pakistan's
Pediatric Heart Heroes

DataNurse is a purpose-built mobile and web application designed for cardiologists and pediatric cardiac surgeons across Pakistan. Children with congenital heart defects require specialized, life-saving surgeries — and every detail of their care matters.

Our platform bridges the gap between mobile data collection at the bedside and centralized management through an admin portal. Doctors can record patient information, surgical procedures, pre- and post-operative data, and track recovery — all in real time.

With DataNurse, hospitals and clinics gain the power of structured healthcare data, enabling better decision-making, audit trails, and improved patient outcomes for Pakistan's youngest patients.

Mission: To digitize and standardize pediatric cardiac surgical records across Pakistan, enabling data-driven improvements in children's heart care.

Mobile App

Capture patient data directly at point of care using our intuitive Android application.

Manage doctors, patients, cases and reports from a powerful web-based control center.

Structured Data

Pakistan-Focused

App Features

Everything You Need to
Manage Cardiac Cases

Patient Registration

Register pediatric patients with complete demographics, medical history, and diagnosis details quickly and accurately.

Surgical Case Records

Document complete surgical details including procedure type, bypass time, aortic clamp time, and intraoperative findings.

Doctor Management

Maintain profiles of surgeons and care teams. Track each doctor's surgical history and outcomes.

Dashboard Analytics

Visual dashboards show active cases, recovery rates, surgical outcomes, and hospital statistics at a glance.

Pre & Post-op Tracking

Record pre-operative assessments and post-operative monitoring including vitals, medications, and recovery milestones.

Reports & Export

Generate comprehensive surgical reports and data exports for research and quality improvement.

Granular permissions ensure doctors see only what they need. Admins control full access securely.

Secure Authentication

JWT-based login with forgot password and reset flows. Hospital data protected and accessible only to authorized users.

Offline-Ready Mobile App

The Android app works in low-connectivity environments — data syncs automatically when connection is restored.

Privacy Policy

Your Data is
Safe With Us

At DataNurse, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of all patient data collected through our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard information. By using the DataNurse app or admin portal, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

We collect patient demographics, medical history, surgical records, and post-operative data entered by authorized healthcare providers. We also collect doctor and user account information for authentication and access control purposes.

How We Use Your Data

Data is used solely for patient care management, surgical outcome tracking, and healthcare quality improvement within your institution. We do not sell, rent, or share patient data with third parties for commercial purposes.

Data Storage & Security

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS and at rest using industry-standard encryption. Access is strictly controlled through role-based permissions. Only authorized hospital staff can view or modify records.

Data Access & Sharing

Patient information is accessible only to verified healthcare professionals at your institution. Data is never shared without explicit consent from the hospital administrator or as required by Pakistani law.

Data Retention

Patient records are retained as required by your institution's policies and applicable Pakistani healthcare regulations. Users may request data deletion in compliance with legal and medical record-keeping requirements.

Contact for Privacy Concerns

If you have questions or concerns about how your data is handled, please reach out to our privacy team. We are committed to resolving any data-related concerns promptly and transparently.
